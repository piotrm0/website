Piotr is a post-doctoral researcher at Carnegie Mellon University's
CyLab . He is working on the problems of algorithmic transparency,
fairness, and privacy in machine learning systems. Piotr completed his
PhD studies at the University of Maryland, College Park and remained
there as a post-doctoral researcher within the Programming Languages
group until 2016. At UMD, he working on the problem of quantifying
information security under varying adversarial assumptions. Piotr's
works appeared in CSF, S&P, CCS, and others.
