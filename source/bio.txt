Piotr is a systems scientist at Carnegie Mellon University, Silicon Valley. He is working on
algorithmic transparency, fairness, and privacy in machine learning systems. Piotr completed his
PhD studies at the University of Maryland, College Park and remained there as a post-doctoral
researcher within the Programming Languages group until 2016. At UMD, he worked on quantifying
information security under varying adversarial assumptions. Piotr's works appeared in S&P, CCS,
CSF, POST, ESOP, and others.
